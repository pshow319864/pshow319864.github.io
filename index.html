About

The Prathibha Sastry Show brings together Entrepreneurial, Adventurous and Innovative men and women in a quest to enable women to dwell deeper on the events that shape their life. An attempt to bring them into focus and enhance not just their life, but also that of those who will watch them share their stories.  

It takes questioning and self-examination to understand oneself - a process that is difficult, but very important, in the pursuit of self-realisation and happiness. To quote Simone de Beauvoir, "Self-knowledge is no guarantee of happiness, but it is on the side of happiness and can supply the courage to fight for it." The Prathibha Sastry Show wants to accompany women on this journey of self-realisation and happiness. 

A show that wants to help women chalk out the blueprint for their business ideas, realise their dream career, be financially independent, mentally strong, spiritually aware and break barriers in both the professional and personal spheres.  

What we have in store

Each season will include 18-22 minutes of relevant content - ordinary men and women and their extraordinary stories.

  • Subject matter experts to help build holistic experiences for oneself.
  • Champions from all verticals to inspire change and the will to act.
  • Entertainers, performers, stars because no experience is complete without some glitz and art.
